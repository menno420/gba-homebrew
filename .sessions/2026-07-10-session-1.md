## 💡 Session idea

Checksum-pin the mirror packages: record the SHA-256 of every
`.pkg.tar.zst/.pkg.tar.xz` the setup script downloads and fail on mismatch.
The leseratte10 mirror is unsigned community infra — pinned hashes won't make
it trusted, but they make it tamper-evident: the trust decision gets made
once, on a verified-working toolchain, instead of silently on every CI run.
Cheap (one array in `tools/setup-toolchain.sh`) and directly shrinks the
lane's biggest standing supply-chain caveat.
