## Guard recipe (for the next session)

Scripting a hazard death headlessly: a wall-hugging descent slides PAST
1-cell wall-adjacent hazards (collide-and-slide keeps the body `mote_half`
off the wall; the falling pass-through window is ~5 frames — lateral drift
can't reach). The reliable deterministic kill: settle the body on a ledge
NEXT to a hazard cluster, then push along the ledge so it slides off into
the hazard (see the PR #9 proof script: RIGHT 400-500 after settling at
depth 26; anchors: `crystal_sensor.overlaps` in `games/lumen-drift/src/main.cpp`,
`--keys` spans in `tools/headless-screenshot.py`). 2-cell clusters exist
partly BECAUSE the 1-cell version was nearly untouchable — if a hazard is
hard to hit in a script, it's weak gameplay too.
