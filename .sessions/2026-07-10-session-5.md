## 💡 Session idea

`gl_save.h` currently stores one payload behind one magic at offset 0. A
cheap next step for save robustness: double-buffered slots (two copies +
sequence byte, load the newer valid one) so a power cut mid-`bn::sram`
write can never corrupt the only copy — SRAM writes are byte-by-byte and
the game writes at the exact moment of death, the most likely rage-off
instant. Headless-verifiable with `--savefile` by corrupting one copy in
the .sav between boots and asserting BEST still loads.
