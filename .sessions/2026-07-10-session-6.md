## Guard recipe (for the next session)

ANY change that lengthens the run-start stall (restart() work: rebake,
asset/system init) shifts EVERY replay span by a constant — diagnose with
the loop-tick watch (`--watch hook:gl_audio_hook:8`, slot 7 vs hardware
frame: lag must be constant after the stall), then re-bisect the offset
against the script's own assertions (same rule as session 5). Audio proofs:
assert hook counters AND mixer nonzero-count together — counters alone
don't prove voicing, the mixer alone doesn't prove which sound; frames
inside a thrust-pattern window conflate voices, so isolate per-sound
evidence in silent-gap frames (death sweep after the frozen world is the
cleanest: it is the only possible voice).
