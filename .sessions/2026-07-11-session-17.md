## Guard recipe (for the next slice)

- **Dawn-route derivation is a scratch pattern, not a tool:** the route
  came from simulating the check-gloam mirror over candidate key-span
  loops and testing ±6-frame alignment shifts (emulator↔ROM frame offset
  is ~2 with occasional single-frame skips — do NOT assume exact
  alignment; design routes with ≥10 px margin). If slice 4 (waves) needs
  richer routes, promote a `tools/`-level recorder like the GBA
  `route-recorder.py` instead of re-deriving inline.
- **Pinned asserts are alignment-sensitive by design:** every
  `--assert-watch` value in `rom-builds.yml` was read from a
  deterministic in-container run of THIS ROM; any gl_sim/main.c change
  legitimately re-pins them (rebuild, re-run, update numbers in the same
  PR) — a mismatch you didn't cause means the build stopped being
  deterministic, which is the real alarm.
- Telemetry slot map lives at the top of `source/main.c` (GL_T_*); slot
  15 pins GL_NIGHT_FRAMES so CI can prove the shipped ROM is full-length.
- The `keypad_update(0)` boot trap (session 16) still applies to every
  new harness invocation; the ROM-side `pad_seen_idle` START guard is in
  main.c — keep both.
