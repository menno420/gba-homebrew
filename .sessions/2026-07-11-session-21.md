## 💡 Session idea

The barricade block rule ("a step may not ENTER a barricade's radius,
but a body already inside may leave") makes every no-trap invariant a
one-line proof: the player is never an argument to the block predicate
(can't be sealed in, by construction), a stagger frame proposes no step
(so it can't chew), and a blocked step always costs the barricade
exactly 1 hp (so a wall is a timer, never a wall). Writing the invariant
into the predicate's SHAPE instead of patching cases afterwards is the
cheapest anti-softlock design this lane has found — reuse it for
lantern-oil (light radius as a predicate, not a special case).
