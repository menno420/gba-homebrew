## 💡 Session idea

When an upgrade plants a new boot-set doc (like docs/SKILLS.md) whose
reachability wiring lives in a template-improved doc, the upgrade verb could
say so explicitly — e.g. `planted: docs/SKILLS.md (reachable only after
--apply-docs applies docs/AGENT_ORIENTATION.md)` — so wave workers know the
plant + apply are one atomic payload instead of discovering it via a
`[reachable]` red on the first strict check.
