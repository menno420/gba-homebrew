## 💡 Session idea

A passive drain CAN be a zero-re-pin feature: split "the state decays"
from "the decay does anything". Slice 7's oil burns on every night
frame — every frame's STATE differs from slice 6 — yet all 177 old
asserts re-ran unchanged, because the drain's only observable effects
(radius shrink + dark press) are gated on a threshold
(`oil < GL_OIL_LOW`) that a fresh tank cannot reach inside any old
proof's window, and the gate is itself host-proven
(`gl_light_radius(oil >= LOW) == R_MAX`, `gl_dark_press(oil >= LOW)
== 0`, plus the static headroom inequality GL_OIL_MAX - 2 nights >=
GL_OIL_LOW as a CI tripwire). When a decay mechanic threatens every
existing pin, don't re-pin — move the pins' whole world INSIDE the
decay's grace period and prove the grace period covers it.
