## 💡 Session idea

Audio can be a ZERO-COST observable if you split "what should sound"
from "making it sound": put the decision layer (cue selection,
ambience tier) in the pure sim as f(state) lookups, mirror it three
ways like any other rule, and let the ARM9 translate decisions into
hardware-channel FIFO writes only on CHANGE (tier flips, cue fires).
Then (1) the trigger chain is pinnable headlessly on EXISTING routes —
this slice added 113 asserts without deriving a single new route,
because the committed oil-press/oil-flask routes already walk every
tier; (2) the frame budget stays flat because the sound generation
runs on the other CPU; and (3) "no binary assets" falls out for free —
a PSG register row IS the sound, so there is nothing to sample and
the ROM stays byte-deterministic without a generator step. The same
trick failed BEFORE it succeeded: even ~30 integer compares of
per-frame glue tipped the measured worst frame 70 -> 71 of 71 lines —
at 1 line of headroom, pay for every new per-frame instruction by
retiring an equal-or-larger per-frame cost (the dawn bar's ~30-cell
reprint bought it back with room to spare, 69/71).
