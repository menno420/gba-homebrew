## 💡 Session idea

Session 28 validated its mirror-side main.c model by reproducing the
carried pins before predicting new ones. This session went one step
stronger, and the step is cheap: **don't hand-pick pins from a model
you trust — diff the model against the emulator over the WHOLE story
first.** `nds-headless-check.py --watch-log` dumps every telemetry
word every frame as CSV; the mirror replay produces the same table;
one script asserted equality over 52,439 values across both full Maw
stories (zero mismatches) BEFORE any pin was chosen. Pin selection
then stops being prediction and becomes row lookup in a proven-equal
table — 62 new asserts landed emulator-green on the first run because
they could not do otherwise. Rule extracted: **a watch-log diff of the
full trajectory is one script and ~30 s; do it before pinning anything
on a new mechanic.**
