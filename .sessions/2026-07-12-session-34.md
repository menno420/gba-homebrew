## 💡 Session idea

Slice 5's Maw carried every prior pin by GATING the new mechanic so no
committed story could trip it (600 quiet frames). Wind can't be gated
by time — it acts on every sailing frame — so this session generalized
the trick: **when a new mechanic must touch every frame, park its
intensity behind a pure seed hash and CHOOSE the salt so every
committed anchor seed maps to the zero-intensity bucket.** One
scripted scan (~19k salt candidates, seconds) found a salt sending all
8 anchor seeds (116/117/118/119/126/127/558/728 — the recorders'
anchors AND scan prefixes AND every carry-water with position pins) to
CALM, whose wind term is *provably identically zero*; the mirror then
asserts the calm-anchor identity as a loud rail, so a future salt or
table retune fails check-brine.py before it silently re-times a route.
Result: a mechanic that touches every ship-step carried all 12 proofs
and all 7 route files with ZERO re-pins. Rule extracted: **the
seed-bucket gate is the time-gate's sibling — pick the constant that
makes the committed corpus provably blind to the new code, and pin
that choice with its own assert.**
