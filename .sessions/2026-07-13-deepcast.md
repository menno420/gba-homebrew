HOLD A to charge a cast (hold time = target depth), lure sinks, seeded fish bite by depth (deeper = heavier = worth more), then the fight: hold A to reel (tension rises, line shortens), release to let the fish run (tension falls, line pays out). Tension maxed = line snaps (lose the fish + one of 3 lures); line reeled to zero = catch, score += weight. Run ends when all 3 lures are gone. Bite-hooking is automatic/forgiving by design — the feel is patience/push-pull, NOT reflex taps (no overlap with Undertow).
