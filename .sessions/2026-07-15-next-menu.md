## 💡 Session idea

**A decision menu is only as honest as its slice counts, and slice
counts are only honest when they cite a completed arc of the same
shape.** Every estimate in the menu is a precedent transfer (Shoal 6,
Gloamline ~11, Undertow 6, Wickroad-cut ≈1 PR each), not a guess — and
writing it that way surfaced a real asymmetry the owner should price
in: NDS titles run ~2x GBA slice counts for the same concept size.
When a future session pitches work to the owner, it should refuse to
write any effort number it cannot pair with a shipped-arc citation;
"how do I know" belongs next to "how much".
