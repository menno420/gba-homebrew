- **A1 — Tinderhand (GBA).** Press-your-luck ember-stoking card game: flip
  cards from a hearth deck (kindling stokes, ash smothers), BANK or STOKE
  after every flip, buy deck-thinning charms between rounds, survive ten
  nights of rising cold. Deck order is a pure `card_of(seed, night, index)`
  function — zero runtime RNG (the Gloamline schedule idiom,
  games/gloamline-nds/source/gl_sim.h@872627d) — so proofs are scripted
  routes with pinned outcomes: committed `proofs.sh` in the Wickroad mold
  (games/wickroad/proofs.sh@872627d), SRAM best via gl_save.h, maxmod audio
  watches (docs/capabilities.md@872627d). No card game among the eleven.
  **~6 slices to v1.0** (Shoal #98–#103 / Courier #96+#105–#108 precedent).
- **A2 — Underroot (NDS, dual-screen native).** Burrow-colony survival where
  the two screens ARE the game: top screen shows the meadow (food patches,
  hawk shadows on a pure f(seed, season, index) schedule); bottom screen is
  the stylus-dug burrow cross-section — nurseries, granaries, forager
  routes; winter is survivable only if the tunnels you drew store enough.
  Exactly the stack Gloamline proved: `--touch` scripting + dual-screen
  screenshots (tools/nds-headless-check.py@872627d), a lockstep host-side
  Python sim mirror as route planner and CI proof (tools/check-gloam.py
  pattern), EEPROM saves via `--battery-in/out`. First colony/digging
  title. **~11 slices to v1.0** (Gloamline arc #50–#75 precedent).
- **A3 — Starloom (browser/PWA).** Daily constellation-weaving puzzle:
  thread one continuous line through a seeded star field within a thread
  budget while drift currents deterministically tug the line; one minute a
  day, share URL, ghost of yesterday's best weave. Provably-solvable seed
  generation (the Tiltstone idiom, games/web-tiltstone/@872627d), side-band
  mulberry32 streams per feature (games/web-undertow/game.js@872627d
  header), real-Chromium smoke (tools/web-smoke-undertow.mjs@872627d mold),
  installable offline via the Drift Garden PWA shell

  tools/package-web-arcade.sh@872627d — and now hosted the moment it lands,
